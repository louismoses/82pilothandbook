# Maneuvers Callouts

## 1. CHANDELLE

- Assume POCS is completed
- Current Altitude(2500) and Heading(360)
- Bank 15 deg
- Full power
- Pitch up 15~20 (maintain airspeed 55-60 knots)
- @45 deg Bank 30 deg established (45)
- @90 deg reduce back to 20 deg
- approaching 180, rollout
- hold (55~60 knots) 5sec
- ease back pressure
- passing 90 knots, reduce power 2100
